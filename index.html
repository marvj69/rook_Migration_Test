About Rook Game Scoring

How to Use: Tap a team to begin bidding, enter the bid and the points, then press "Submit." Keep track of all round scores in the “History” section. You can also freeze a current game, view saved games, start a new game, view statistics, toggle dark mode, and change the theme from the menu.

What's New in 1.3.5:

version 1.3.5 introduces a better way to handle team names and statistics.

- Sandbagger. Are you a sandbagger? Check the team stats to find out.
- Select teams: Team statistics save per team once team names are entered.
- Automatic Team Stats: Newly created team names are stored locally, along with more stats.
- Expanded Statistics Section: Click the dropdown for more stats.
- Various small UI and logic improvements.
